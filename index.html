Our Features

The vast majority of the ski resorts have been developed in the open air, and adjacent to mountains, and villages. However, there are many indoor ski resorts e.g. Harbin Wanda Indoor Ski and Winter Sports Resort in China that have drawn international attention. Likewise, Ski Dubai is also a popular indoor ski resort. Interestingly, some of the indoor ski resorts have the potential to banish the need for people to visit the mountains altogether! Certainly, ski resorts attract international tourists; and therefore, make a good contribution to the local and national economies. However, many critics argue that that the development of ski resorts impact on the natural ecosystem and have contributed to the urbanisation of mountainous areas in many parts of the world.
